About Me

Hi! I’m El, a freelance makeup artist based in Sampaloc, Manila. I started my makeup journey in 2023 and specialize in creating beautiful looks for special occasions. I provide home service makeup for events such as graduations, birthdays, and other celebrations. My goal is to enhance my clients’ natural beauty and help them feel confident and glamorous for their special moments.
